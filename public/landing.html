What Hari does

A concierge, not a search box

Hari reasons about the recipient and occasion, asks one smart question instead of a form, and never bluffs about what it found.

💬

Natural conversation

Understands occasion, recipient and feeling — by text or voice — in English, Sinhala or Tamil. No forms, no filters to fiddle with.

🎯

One smart question

Budget once, then a single taste cue. Never an interrogation.

🛡️

Honest by design

Every reply is grounded in real Kapruka results — no relabelling fruit baskets as ramen.

🧠

Remembers people

Saves recipients, birthdays, anniversaries, and your gifting style.

🛒

Real checkout

Cart, wishlist, and a genuine order with a secure pay link.

💙

Emotionally aware

Handles apologies, condolences and get-well moments with warmth — and picks up past chats where you left off.

🎙️

Voice-first when you want it

Tap the mic, speak naturally, hear replies back. Switch languages mid-conversation.
