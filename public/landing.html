Under the hood

An agent built on guardrails, not vibes

The hard part of agents isn't capability — it's discipline: knowing when to ask, refusing to bluff, respecting a strict time budget, and staying graceful in three languages on serverless infrastructure.

🧪 Result grounding

Extracts the recipient's interests and the items explicitly requested, then verifies the results actually contain them — re-searching or admitting the miss instead of relabelling. No more "ramen gift basket" over fruit baskets.

⚧ Gender & safety filters

Infers recipient gender across all three languages to drop opposite-gender items, hides out-of-stock products, and strips NSFW & category-facet noise — deterministically, never emptying the list.

⏱️ Serverless-safe i18n

Anchors one request-wide time budget so translation + DB loads can't blow the 60s limit, runs inbound translation concurrently with the Supabase load, and degrades gracefully instead of 502-ing.

🚦 Intent guardrails

Budget asked once per chat, one taste question max, "pick the best" recommends without re-searching, and the agent is forced to actually search when it only promises options.
